"IT'S NOT ENOUGH TO HAVE IDEAS,WE NEED TO BELIEVE IN THEM AND MAKE THEM HAPPEN"

ASHISH RAJ

Contesting for the post of

VICE-PRESIDENT

STUDENT'S GYMKHANA

INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY PATNA

MANIFESTOS

1.  IMPROVING CAMPUS LIFE      

  • E - Rickshaw :  I propose to start up E-Rickshaw service in the campus with charging stations provided to them. This would foster a cheaper conveyance in and out of the campus contrary to the exorbitant price the students currently pay especially to go out of the campus.  
  • Market : The market facility needs a boost with a greater variety of shops like a tailor shop, shoe store with cobbler facility, a bookstore to name a few, in order to meet the needs of campus junta. At the same time I will ensure a rightful allocation of these shops and a code of conduct within which they operate so that the students don't face any exploitation from the vendors.
  • A price regulation and code of conduct for the working canteen is required with proper penalty in case of shortcomings. There have been a few instances where the food served is unhygienic that does not justify the high prices that we end up paying.
  • There is a need to diversify the food items available at the canteens and night canteen with setting up of food joints at some strategic locations. I wish to setup an independent student body that would work in coordination with Student Gymkhana to ensure the achievement of the above objectives. I also look forward to setting up subsidized food joints like McD ,CCD and a campus restaurant.​
  • Cycle pools : A very innovative and effective step which many campuses have adopted is setting up cycle pools where in the institute would place few cycles at all key locations accessible to all. It is about time that IIT P also has the facilities at par or better than other IITs 
  • Cycle sheds  : We come to the campus with a new cycle but by the end of the semester the cycles are barely in a working condition because their are no sheds to guard them against heat and rain. During my term, I would ensure that the cycle shed is setup without any further delay.
  • Subsidized Reservation Counter : Railway is a popular for of conveyance at IIT P. Most of the students travel via train for various reasons. For the same I wish to set up an authorized reservation counter of Railways that will provide students with tickets at a concession.
  • Souvenir shop : There isn't a greater pride than wearing your college hoodie and moving around. I aim to setup a souvenir shop for the sale of college merchandise in proper manner without any exploitation. It would also allow us to have the official logo of the institute on the merchandise. 
  • College Website: I am looking forward to host a well maintained website with the online presence of all clubs, gymkhana and fests. For this I will propose the institute to host the websites and we students would maintain it. 

2.  PLACEMENT AND ENTREPRENEURIAL ACTIVITES

Full time Placements and Startups are two of the major career option for the students at IIT  P. The Training and Placement Cell caters to full time placement and internships to the students of the college. Hence it is on my priority list to modify and augment the momentum of Placement Cell's functioning along with promoting entrepreneurial activities

There is an urgent requirement of installation of a proper infrastructure for the Placement Cell to function smoothly. Its efficient working requires a revamped hierarchical structuring and increased student involvement. At present the duty allotment to the students in the Cell's activities is not very distinct.  As an active member of the current Placement Cell team, I propose creating new position of responsibility in the Placement Cell with clear definition of duties and hierarchical structure inviting participation from all the disciplines of UG and PG courses.

I aim to enhance the infrastructure of the existing Placement facility by incorporating the following:
  • Increase the number of companies on campus by converting more companies interested to hire from various branches.
  • Increase the number of  PG oriented companies on campus for ensuring higher placement ratio and good career for all.
  • Proper infrastructure to conduct Group discussions and Personal Interviews.
  • Facility of Tele Conferencing and Video Conferencing within Placement Cell.​
  • Round the clock assistance by the students' volunteers at each level of placement process.
  • Highly motivated and experienced staff to coordinate the whole process.
  • Ensure sufficient funds allocation and utilization to the cell to enable smooth functioning and achieving the aforementioned objectives. 
  •  At the same time I would do all in my capacity to promote entrepreneurial activities on campus  by coordinating with the E-Club.

3. ALUMNI RELATIONS

  • I believe that Alumni network is indispensable for the growth and development of the college. But we have been lacking on this part since long. However  a praiseworthy step has been taken by our seniors in laying the foundation of SAAR - a body to look after Alumni relations.I will do everything  in my capacity to ensure proper spawning and development of SAAR into a strong body that keeps us in connect with our Alumni  members and the alumni to their alma mater.
  • As a part of SAAR  I look forward to ensuring channel wherein our Alumni members can guide us and become path showers. At the same time they can be honored for their achievements that they make.
  • I also intend to have an Annual Alumni Meet to serve as platform for get together  and mutual exchange of ideas.

4.  STUDENT GYMKHANA GOVERNANCE

  • I wish to ensure a complete transparency in the working Gymkhana.For the purpose of same I propose that all meetings and decisions taken would be well documented and made public for the junta to be aware of what is going on. I further wish to involve the junta to put agendas either through their elected representative or in person.
  • To ensure accountability of the office bearers I propose a monthly score based feedback and suggestions on the elected representative as well as proper guidelines on penalties imposed if found not working. I also wish to introduce certificates of  holding the office that office bearers hold which will be provided at the end of the term and only to the deserving ones who have  actually delivered their duties well. This will not only act as impetus for working diligently  but also serve as penalty to the defaulters. Again this decision will be based on the feedback from the people and not arbitrary opinions of a few.
  • The year long events must be planned well in advance and suitable dates and budget of the gymkhana should be passed in the beginning of the session

5.  HOSTEL DEVELOPMENT AND HALL CULTURE

  • Setting up of common meeting place accessible to girls too for common meetings well equipped with Wifi, projector and other needful things in the ground floor of the boys hostel.
  • Further there are serious lapses every now and then in terms of hygiene and water. I wish to employ a proper one shot solution to it by marking the calendar dates in advance for fogging and purifier maintenance and see to it that the deadlines are kept.
  • I also propose pay and eat mess service where in student only pay for the meals that take and in choice of their mess. A digital approach is required for it and I wish to undertake this.
  • There is also a need to upkeep the common room and gym.For the gym, a trainer is necessary so that one can benefit out of it. Further the obsolete  equipments need to be replaced and maintained
  • With new hostels being under construction and soon ready to live and in years to come with more strength I believe it is time to lay foundation of inter hall events and general championship trophy.Though at present we have one boys and one girls hostel, this can be carried out in blocks and when the hostels are ready it can take the shape of inter hall events.

6.  EQUALITY OF FREEDOMS TO THE GIRLS AND BOYS

  • It is disheartening to see that the girls do not enjoy equal priviledges as do the boys. Their freedom and priviledges are curtailed at several stages and often when they speak up for it, it goes unheard to the ears of the college admin. But it shall not be with me. The idea of Student Gymkhana is ensures that needs and requirements of the entire student body is represented to the admin and fight for any injustices and short-comings without any compromise on their needs. 
  • I don't see any rational behind the 12o'clock deadline for the girls whereas no such things for boys. Moreover advocating such a thing on grounds of security is insane. I believe our  campus is a wonderful and safer  place than any other place around. If some feel that it is not safe then the right thing to do would be to make it SAFE and not impose restrictions just because you don't wish to take up any responsibilty. I will not tolerate this kind of practice and will make all efforts to do away with it to extent of going ahead with all necessary arrangements like camera or deployment of security guards at all strategic locations. This deadline not only imposes restrictions on the right of girls but also has serious impact on their career and work. People have their own hours when they work at their best efficiency at night time being the choice for the most.But due to the deadline the girls cannot work late night in labs and forced to give up the continuity if their work.
  • Availing in Boys  Hostel mess :  Many girls  especially PG girls may be skipping their meals due to avoid going so far to their hostel and even if they manage they may be getting very  little time for the lunch. Though this may not be a problem once the girls hostel is ready. Inspite of that I propose that the Boys mess should be made available to the girls too. A proper mechanism to get that to work will also be worked out.
  • A dedicated bus service for girls is indeed a necessity especially at peek times and I wish to provide this.
  • The access to the courts especially badminton court is again in many ways constrained and cubersome to avail by the girls.This is serious issue and I wish to  make  the process as seamless as it is for boys that is their  are no restrictions in what so ever which way! At the same time the courts would be independently setup in the upcoming girls hostel.

7.  PG - UG RELATIONSHIP

The kind of stressed relationship that the UG - PG students share  is well known in the campus. It is also known that it is not right to have bitter feelings against one another not only at this level but also on levels of regionalism or branch. I wish to work towards improving this by mutual discussion and talks because often conflicts are outcomes of difference in perspective and misunderstandings which requires an intervention of the third person to sort things out. I believe the post of VP in every capacity bears this responsibility and I promise to intervene and take up what is right in any such case. This effort would definitely improve overall campus life.  

8.  SPORTS

  • Sports  Infrastructure :  Despite completing 10 years, it is pathetic to see the condition of the courts and fields. The Inter-IIT team sheds its blood and sweat but due to infrastructural limitations their performance and our reputation suffers. I not only propose the standardization of the courts but will also look into the matter that the tremendous expenditure carried out in the name of Sports is actually utilized properly. 
  • Sports complex : There is an urgent need of proper sports complex ensuring the right environment for the play of any sport
  • Infinito : Lay more emphasis on the Sports Fest Infinito in order for it to not only grow into bigger but also a celebrated event of the year. The events will be carried out in more organised and systematic fashion.The rules would be clear in order to ensure fair play. The organised events should and must be played in the spirit of sportsmanship else it defeats the purpose of having such an event.
  • It is equally important to have full time trained coaches to train the team well as it is wisely said " A good coach knows what is the best for you".
  • Apart from Inter-IIT team, for general promotion of sporting activities, I wish to initiate a proper channel which would facilitate provision of equipments, dedicated ground hours and coach attention to amateurs who wish to take up particular sport.
  • REST-ROOMS :  Juice stall, first-aid and Drinking Water again is needed at all the courts.For this I want to set up a small  restroom one near BB and VB court and one near the Football and cricket pitch that will have the facilites of first-aid, water cooler,changing rooms shower facility,vending machines serving liquids and injury room for any injured person to rest before taken to the HOSPITAL.
  • INTER-IIT : Finally, I would vouch for proper utilisation of INTER-IIT funds and if need be request for more to ensure good quality equipments, accessories, wearables and most importantly, the FOOD. A comfortable journey to is necessary and not luxury.

9.  TECHNICAL

  • I also wish to raise the standards of technical involvements of the campus students. For this purpose I propose that in upcoming Gymkhana building all the clubs will be given space to carry out their activities as well as a justified budget will be provided from Gymkhana funds.
  • Special efforts will be made to strengthen Celesta with diversification of events supported by justified budget.
  • Throughout the year activities and sessions would be planned and conducted by the clubs and gymkhana would support these activities. 

10. CULTURAL

  • Music Club is in growing stage and I will do all in my capacity see it grow.
  • A proper music room, dance room, theater practice room would be built and designed in upcoming Gymkhana Building for the promotion of the cultural activities in a professional manner.
  • The activities and events like Nebula and Reverb  under HOSCA is already doing well but there is no limit to excellence and I would love to extend all my support to try out new things that would take the Cultural involvement of our college to even greater heights.
  • ANWESHA : the most celebrated event in our college and every passing it evolves into something better and I wish to carry the legacy forward and do every possible thing to contribute in its evergrowing success. 

11.  OVERALL PERCEPTION

  • Will surely assess the reason behind continuous fall in the Institute Ranking and take all needed steps to make up for our shortcomings. This will be a collective effort which would comprise equal contribution from the entire IIT-P fraternity.
  • It is my vision to see our college compared and considered at par with any other IIT no matter how old it may be !
  • And surely there is no bigger agenda than students and I promise that all my actions will be for the good of student body as a whole.

  

ANY FURTHER SUGGESTIONS AND INPUTS ARE WELCOMED.
